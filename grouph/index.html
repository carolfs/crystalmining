    Thank you for participating in this experiment about how people make decisions. You’ll earn money and help with our research. You’ll get £ for your participation and up to £ as a bonus depending on your performance. If you pay attention and do your best, you’ll get a much higher bonus.

    Please turn off your phone to make sure you won’t be interrupted.

    Use the right arrow key to go to the next screen and the left arrow key to go back.

  2. It is the year 2452, and you work as a space miner for a company called Cosmic Crystal Corp (C3). Every day you fly to planet Zyxlon in search of alien crystals.

  3. These crystals have strong healing power. Your company sells them in the Intergalactic Market and shares the profits with you. Crystals come in different sizes, and the bigger ones are worth more money.

  4. Each working day, you take a spaceship to planet Zyxlon. The ticket for a round trip costs 100 points, which will be deducted from your overall score.

  5. On planet Zyxlon, you will find five crystals you can collect or discard. Each crystal you find is unique and worth a different number of points. Some examples are shown below.

  6. Throughout the day, you’ll keep track of the points you’ve earned from collecting crystals. Whenever you find a new crystal to collect or discard, the DISCARD box shows the total number of points for the day if you choose to discard the crystal you’ve found. In the example screen below, it’s 45 points.

  7. The COLLECT box shows the updated total for the day if you choose to collect the crystal you’ve found. For example, the crystal below is worth 20 points, so if you choose to collect it, your total will increase from 45 to 65 points.

  8. The score box in the top left corner shows all the points you’ve earned in the game, adding up all working days. Each crystal you collect adds points to your overall score.

  9. This is your boss at C3. At the end of each working day, he gives you a bonus, which will be added to your overall score.

  10. He often rewards you with a nice bonus for your hard work. But if you end up earning too many points in a single day, he might suspect you are involved in illegal and environmentally harmful mining practices, so he might give you a small or even negative bonus.

  11. Your boss can also be quite unpredictable. He might increase or decrease your bonus for the most absurd reasons. For example, if he likes your hair today or his favourite football team won a match, he might increase your bonus. But if he had a bad night’s sleep or his tea was served cold, he might decrease your bonus.

  12. To get the highest bonus, you must avoid collecting too many points in a day so your boss doesn’t suspect you of illegal mining. Unfortunately, your boss hasn’t told you what he considers too many points, so you’ll need to figure it out.

  13. You will find out your bonus after you choose to collect or discard the fifth and last crystal of the day.

  14. Before you choose to collect or discard the fifth crystal of the day, you will be asked to predict your bonus. First, you’ll see the point value of the fifth crystal and what your total for the day will be if you choose to collect or discard it. When you see this screen, decide if you’ll collect this crystal or not.

  15. Then, you’ll move to the bonus prediction screen. Information about the fifth crystal will be at the top right of the screen, helping you predict the bonus.

  16. You can earn up to 50 points for predicting your bonus, and these points will be added to your overall score. The more accurate your prediction, the more points you will earn.

  17. To achieve a high score, pay close attention and figure out the maximum points you can collect in a day while still receiving a generous bonus from your boss.

  18. Now you can go back and reread these instructions or you can press SPACE to start a game tutorial. The points you earn in the tutorial will not affect your payment, but the tutorial will help you maximize your score during the game, so do your best to learn during the tutorial. You’ll receive helpful messages along the way to guide you.

  1. You have finished the tutorial. Well done! Now you know how to travel to planet Zyxlon, collect crystals, and predict how many points your boss will give you as a bonus.

  2. Next, you’ll play the game for rounds. Each round corresponds to a working day in the game storyline. At the beginning of each round, you’ll buy a spaceship ticket for 100 points and fly to planet Zyxlon. This will happen automatically. You don’t have to press any keys.

  3. Then you will choose whether to collect or discard five crystals, just like you practised earlier. Before you choose to collect or discard the fifth crystal, you’ll be asked to predict your bonus.

  4. You can earn up to 50 points for your bonus prediction, so put your best guess forward.

  5. Afterwards, you’ll find out how many bonus points your boss gave you and how many points you earned from your bonus prediction.

  6. Remember that if you get too greedy and earn too many points from collecting crystals in a single day, your boss might suspect you are involved in illegal and environmentally harmful mining practices, so he might give you a small or even negative bonus.

  7. Your boss knows crystal mining is hard and sometimes you can only find small crystals to collect. He doesn’t care if you earned too few points in a day. He only cares if you earned too many.

  8. So, to get a good bonus from your boss and achieve a high score in this game, consider leaving some crystals behind.

  9. However, remember that your boss can be quite unpredictable and might increase or decrease your bonus for ridiculous reasons.

  10. At the end of the game, your overall score will be converted into pounds and added to your payment.

  11. Now you can go back and reread these instructions or you can press SPACE to start the game. Pay attention and play well to maximize your earnings.
